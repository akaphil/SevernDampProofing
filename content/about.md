## About Severn Damp Proofing

Severn Damp Proofing Ltd is a Stroud-based team with over **35 years' experience** treating damp in properties across Gloucestershire and the Cotswolds. We handle rising damp, penetrating damp, condensation, basement and cellar tanking, and timber treatment.

### A Different Kind of Damp Company

The damp-proofing industry doesn't have the best reputation — too many firms reach for an expensive chemical "solution" regardless of what's actually wrong. We built our business on the opposite approach:

We know Gloucestershire's housing — from modern estates with condensation problems to solid-walled Victorian and Cotswold-stone properties with penetrating damp. That experience means an accurate diagnosis and the right fix the first time.
