### Rising Damp Treatment

Genuine rising damp — moisture drawn up through the wall from the ground — is far less common than the industry suggests, but when it's real it needs a proper remedy. We install replacement damp-proof courses by injection and replaster with salt-resistant systems, all guaranteed. [Read more about rising damp →](/rising-damp/)

### Penetrating Damp

Water getting in through the building fabric: failed pointing, cracked render, leaking gutters or downpipes, or bridged cavities. Often the most straightforward damp to cure once the entry point is correctly identified — and frequently mistaken for rising damp by less thorough surveyors.

### Condensation & Mould Control

The most common cause of damp in modern, well-sealed homes. Warm, moist air meeting cold surfaces leads to black mould around windows, in corners and behind furniture. We diagnose the cause and recommend practical fixes — improved ventilation, extraction, insulation and environmental changes — rather than treating the symptoms alone.

### Basement & Cellar Tanking

Waterproofing below-ground spaces with cementitious tanking or cavity-drain membrane systems, turning damp cellars into dry, usable rooms. [Read more about basement tanking →](/basement-tanking/)

### Timber Treatment

Damp and timber problems go hand in hand. We treat **woodworm** (wood-boring beetle), **wet rot** and **dry rot**, tackling both the outbreak and the underlying moisture that caused it — because timber decay is a symptom of a damp problem that also needs solving.
